WHY MASTER HEAT TECH

From Master Heat Tech’s desire to get closer to its customers located in the vibrant MENA region comes Master Heat Tech HEI Industries. Master Heat Tech Industries manufactures a complete line of heating elements for the industrial sector.

ABOUT
01

Mission

Master Heat Tech Industries was born with the intent of the Master Heat Tech group to be close to its customers in the MENA region. With a comprehensive catalog and the support of 20 years of experience in the heating industry we aim to offer customer-oriented service from custom design to delivery of the finished product.

02

Vision

Attention to detail, curiosity for challenge and a desire to innovate are what drive us every day to seek new heating solutions for our customers and the industrial heating sector.

03

Values

We aim to offer our customers cutting-edge solutions for their heating projects through flexibility, efficiency and sustainability.
