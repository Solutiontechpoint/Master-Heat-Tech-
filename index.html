WHY CALORFLEX

From Calorflex’s desire to get closer to its customers located in the vibrant MENA region comes Calorflex HEI Industries. Calorflex HEI Industries manufactures a complete line of heating elements for the industrial sector.

ABOUT
01

Mission

Calorflex HEI Industries was born with the intent of the Calorflex group to be close to its customers in the MENA region. With a comprehensive catalog and the support of 20 years of experience in the heating industry we aim to offer customer-oriented service from custom design to delivery of the finished product.

02

Vision

Attention to detail, curiosity for challenge and a desire to innovate are what drive us every day to seek new heating solutions for our customers and the industrial heating sector.

03

Values

We aim to offer our customers cutting-edge solutions for their heating projects through flexibility, efficiency and sustainability.
